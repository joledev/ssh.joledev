# Pressure
date: 2026-03-27

No matter how many interviews I face, the human feeling
of not being good enough never fades. It feels heavy knowing that one question,
one wrong statement, one doubt can disqualify you entirely.

As if my worth and who I am could be shown in a 30-minute video call
because we're all in a hurry.

When did we normalize that 30 minutes are enough to talk about ourselves?
